About SSB Future Officers

Forging India's Next Generation of Military Leaders

Our Legacy

Established in 2012, SSB Future Officers emerged from the visionary leadership of our CEO, Col. Anuj Yadav (Retd). As a former Psychologist Officer who has assessed the personality profiles of thousands of candidates, he established this platform to provide aspirants with expert guidance grounded in real-world psychological selection criteria. What began as his specialized mentorship sessions has evolved into India's premier SSB preparation platform, trusted by successful officers across all three services.

Our Mission: Officer Development

Our mission transcends mere test preparation. We are committed to developing the officer-like qualities that selection boards seek. We demystify the SSB assessment process while cultivating leadership, decision-making, and ethical frameworks essential for military command. We believe every candidate possesses officer potential – our role is to reveal and refine it.

Elite Advisory Board

SSB Future Officers is guided by a distinguished panel of former SSB presidents, psychologists, and serving officers. Our team includes military psychologists with decades of assessment experience, retired service members who understand command expectations, and education specialists who develop scientifically validated preparation methodologies.

Chief Mentor: Col. Anuj Yadav (Retd)

Leading our team is Col. Anuj Yadav (Retd), a veteran with over 32 years of distinguished service in the Indian Army. As a former Senior Psychologist at SSB Bangalore, he brings invaluable insider knowledge of the psychological assessment process. His direct experience in analyzing psychometric tests ensures that our training methodologies are not just theoretical, but strictly aligned with the current psychological frameworks utilized by the Services Selection Board.

Proven Track Record of Excellence

Our greatest validation comes from our alumni – now serving as Captains, Majors, Flight Lieutenants, and Lieutenants across India's armed forces. Their consistent success across various selection boards demonstrates the effectiveness of our methodology. We maintain an active mentorship program where our alumni contribute to refining our preparation systems.

Strategic Institutional Partnerships

Beyond individual preparation, we collaborate with premier defense coaching institutes, military schools, and university NCC units. Our institutional licensing program ensures standardized, high-quality preparation methodologies reach aspiring officers nationwide. We work closely with educators to align our materials with evolving SSB assessment patterns.

Research & Development

We maintain a dedicated R&D division that continuously analyzes SSB selection trends, psychological assessment methodologies, and leadership development frameworks. Our materials are regularly updated based on feedback from recent candidates and insights from serving officers, ensuring relevance and effectiveness.
